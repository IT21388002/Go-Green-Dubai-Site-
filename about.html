About Us...

WELCOME TO GO GREEN EXPORTS…

Go Green Exports Pvt Ltd was established for manufacturing and exporting coir-related products, and our brand name has become a prominent symbol in the global market. We currently export our high-quality coir products to over 50 countries worldwide. As one of the leading manufacturers and exporters in Sri Lanka, we hold honored certifications including 9001:2015 and OMRI, which validate the superior quality of our coir products. We known for our quality and exceptional service within the industry developed over the years of sustainability in quality and supply. In 2023, we have won the gold award in coconut and coconut products sector under large category awarded by National Chamber of Exporters in Sri Lanka. As a team of professionals, expertise in coconut industry, we take pride in our commitment to delivering high-quality products at the best prices. As an experienced logistic partner, we also always make sure to deliver on time.

With an aim to provide eco-friendly solutions for agricultural and industrial sector while contributing to the betterment of the Coconut plantations in Sri Lanka, we are a company who offer a range of coir products to meet the international standards and market needs. Our product ranges from Cocopeat Blocks, Coco Peat Briquette (Compressed brick), Coco Peat Loose Bag (Retail Bag), Coco Husk Chips (Blocks), Hydroponic Grow Bags / Coir Grow Bags, Coir Disc, Coco Husk Chips (Briquettes), Planter Bag (Quick fill bag), Coco Peat and Chips for Pets, Coir Pots, Weed Mats and more. Utilizing the latest technology with a team of well-qualified professionals in the industry, we ensure a premium quality product each and every time. We process the best raw materials and run tests and do quality checks at each stage to make sure that every product that leaves our manufacturing plant is up to the accepted export standards.

Go Green Exports (Pvt) Ltd is registered for export purposes under esteemed regulatory bodies such as the Export Development Board, Sri Lanka Port Authority, and the Coconut Development Authority. To maintain strict quality standards and monitor the cargo at every step of the exporting process, we have a team of qualified export specialists. With years of experience in the coir industry, we have earned a reputation as a reliable solution provider for all growing challenges.

At Go Green Exports (Pvt) Ltd, our vision revolves around our unwavering commitment to product quality, constant innovations benefiting our users, and utmost respect for the planet. We aspire to not just meet but exceed expectations, with the ultimate reward being the smiles we bring to our customers’ faces.
