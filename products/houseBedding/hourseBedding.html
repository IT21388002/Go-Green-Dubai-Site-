HOURSE BEDDING & TERRARIUM

Coco Peat and Coco Chips serve as exceptional bedding materials for pets and reptiles, offering a natural and comfortable environment. These materials are particularly suited for a variety of animals, including lizards, tortoises, snakes, iguanas, turtles, and frogs, providing them with a healthy and cushioned living space.

Advantages of Coco Bedding:

1. Moisture Absorption and Softness: Coco Chips are especially effective as floor layers due to their excellent moisture absorption and softness, which provide a cushioned and comfortable surface for animals. This softness ensures that the bedding is safe and gentle, without any sharp edges that could harm the animals.

2. Natural and Organic Composition: Made from 100% natural and organic coco peat and coco husk, this bedding material is free from artificial additives. It provides a safe and healthy environment for pets, maintaining their well-being and comfort.

3. Waste Reduction:Coco bedding contributes to waste reduction due to its biodegradable nature. It is easy to dispose of, making it an eco-friendly choice for pet owners and reptile enthusiasts.

4. Health Benefits: The natural substances in coco peat are particularly beneficial for animals, especially for snakes, as they help promote muscle growth. This makes coco bedding not just a comfort solution, but also a health-enhancing one.

5. Customizable and Versatile: Coco bedding can be ordered in any custom size to suit both retail and wholesale needs. It is available with required packaging, making it a versatile option for a wide range of applications, from small terrariums to larger enclosures.

Coco Peat and Coco Chips bedding is an ideal solution for those seeking a natural, comfortable, and sustainable bedding material for their pets and reptiles. Whether used in terrariums or as general animal bedding, this product offers a healthy and environmentally friendly option that ensures the safety and well-being of various animals.
