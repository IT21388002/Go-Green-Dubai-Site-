COIR BLANKET

Go Green Coir Mat Blankets are a natural and effective solution for soil erosion control. These blankets are made from 100% coconut fiber, with either photodegradable or biodegradable grids stitched to the top and bottom layers for enhanced durability. The coir fiber is held in place by polypropylene (PP) netting, which is reinforced with stitched yarn, providing additional strength and stability.

Advantages of Go Green Coir Mat Blankets:

1. Erosion Control and Seed Protection: Coir blankets prevent soil erosion by shielding seeds and stabilizing the soil until vegetation is firmly established. The pre-seeded mats help reduce soil loss from wind and rain forces, allowing plants to grow without soil compaction or crusting.

2. Enhanced Vegetative Growth: By moderating moisture levels and temperature, coir blankets promote faster vegetative development. As the blankets gradually decompose, they enrich the soil, further supporting plant growth.

3. Adaptability to Extreme Conditions:Designed for steep slopes, high-discharge channels, and areas needing permanent protection, coir blankets offer reinforcement and erosion control for sites with severe erosion risks. Their durability makes them suitable for challenging environmental conditions.

4. Biodegradability and Environmental Impact: Coir blankets are fully biodegradable, breaking down over time to become part of the soil, thus supporting sustainable erosion control. The product's eco-friendliness aligns with the growing public awareness of environmental conservation.

Applications for Go Green Coir Mat Blankets:

◼ Erosion Control for Slopes and Channels: Ideal for sites with un-vegetated steep slopes or drainage ways, coir blankets provide long-term soil stabilization and erosion prevention.

◼ Pre-Seeded Options: The coir blankets can be pre-seeded with specific seeds based on the location, making them ideal for landscaping, restoration projects, and agricultural settings.

◼ High-Risk Erosion Sites:Developed for areas with extreme slopes or significant soil loss risks, coir blankets offer reliable protection and aid in revegetation.

Customizable Specifications:

Coir blankets are available in various thicknesses based on specific application requirements. They are supplied in roll form, with widths of up to 2.4 meters and lengths of up to 50 meters. Depending on the project size and shipping container load capacity, suitable lengths can be proposed. Each roll is wrapped in impermeable polypropylene for protection during transport and storage.

Site Preparation:
◼ Grading and Soil Preparation: Grade and shape the installation area. Loosen 2 to 3 inches of topsoil, ensuring the surface is free from obstructions like rocks or wood for optimal contact with the blanket.

Seeding:
◼ Pre-Installation Seeding: In most cases, it is recommended to seed the soil before installing the coir blanket. For turf reinforcement and open weave matting, seeding may be done after installation. For best results, rake a light layer of soil over the seeded surface.

Anchoring:
◼ Using Staples or Stakes:Anchor the blankets with U-shaped metal staples (minimum 11-gauge, 6" to 12" long) or wooden stakes (at least 12" long). Maintain a 2" distance between the blanket's edge and the center of the staple or stake for secure installation.

Go Green Coir Mat Blankets offer an environmentally friendly and efficient solution for long-term soil stabilization and erosion control, supporting sustainable land management practices across various challenging environments.
