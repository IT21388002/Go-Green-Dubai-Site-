200L LOW COMPRESS BAG

200L Low Compress Bags are a versatile growing medium made from coco peat, tailored for various horticultural and agricultural applications. These bags are produced by adjusting the mixture of coir fiber and other materials, such as coco chips or crushed coco products, to meet specific crop requirements. The unique feature of these bags is their low compression, which makes them easy to loosen while maintaining an optimal moisture content of 25-35%.

Advantages of 200L Low Compress Bags:

1. Customizable Growing Medium: The mixture of coco peat and coir fiber in these bags can be customized by increasing or decreasing the percentage of each component. This flexibility allows for the creation of a growing medium that is perfectly suited to the needs of different crops. Additionally, coco chips or crushed materials can be added to enhance the mixture's properties.

2. Easy to Handle and Maintain: The lower compression of these bags means they are easier to loosen and prepare for planting. This characteristic, combined with their ability to maintain the ideal moisture content, makes them a user-friendly option for growers of all scales.

3. Wide Range of Applications:These bags are highly versatile and can be used as a soil substitute in various applications, including potting mixtures, landscaping, seed generation, and home gardening. They are particularly popular in golf course maintenance, mushroom farming, bonsai cultivation, vegetable beds, and rose cultivation.

4. Soil Conditioning and Enhancement: As a soil substitute, 200L Low Compress Bags are excellent for soil conditioning, helping to improve soil structure and water retention. This makes them an ideal choice for enhancing the growth environment in a variety of settings.

5. Convenient Packaging: The bags are packed in white poly bags, which not only protect the contents during transportation but also make handling and storage easier. This ready-made product is designed for ease of use, requiring minimal effort to prepare and apply.

200L Low Compress Bags are a practical and efficient growing medium, suitable for a wide range of horticultural and agricultural uses. Their customizable nature, ease of use, and suitability for various applications make them a preferred choice for professional growers and home gardeners alike.
