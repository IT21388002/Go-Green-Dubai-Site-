OIL ABSORBENT

Coco peat is an effective and eco-friendly solution for cleaning both hazardous and non-hazardous spills. This biodegradable material does not contain harmful dust and is capable of safely absorbing a wide range of liquids. Its lightweight nature allows for easy spreading over spilled areas, making it a practical and time-saving medium for spill clean-up operations. Coco peat's absorbed material can be composted, further enhancing its environmental sustainability. GO GREEN produces dry oil absorbents in loose bags, ready for use in various spill situations.

Advantages of Using Coco Peat as an Oil Absorbent:

1. Biodegradable and Environmentally Safe: Coco peat is a natural, eco-friendly absorbent that does not introduce harmful substances into the environment and can be safely composted after use.

2. Lightweight and Easy to Use: Its lightweight nature makes coco peat easy to handle, spread, and clean up, significantly reducing labor time and effort.

3. High Absorption Capacity:Coco peat efficiently absorbs a variety of liquids, including hazardous substances, making it suitable for industrial, agricultural, and domestic spill management.

4. Time-Saving: The quick absorption rate of coco peat allows for fast clean-up, minimizing the time required to manage spills and reducing downtime.

Recommended Applications for Coco Peat Oil Absorbent:

◼ Fuel Spills (Diesel or Petrol): Highly effective for absorbing fuel spills, providing a safe and efficient clean-up solution.

◼ Lubricants and Solvents: Ideal for mineral vegetable lubricants, transmission fluids, and solvents, ensuring thorough absorption and easy disposal.

◼ Cooking Oils and Water-Based Chemicals:Suitable for kitchen environments and industrial settings to manage spills of cooking oils and non-hazardous chemicals.

◼ Mild Corrosives and Organic Waste: Can be used to absorb mild corrosive substances and liquid organic waste, offering a versatile clean-up tool across various industries.

◼ Pesticides and Herbicides: An excellent medium for handling spills related to agricultural chemicals, providing a safe and environmentally sound clean-up method.

Coco peat absorbents offer an efficient, sustainable solution for managing a wide range of spills, ensuring safety while supporting environmental responsibility.
