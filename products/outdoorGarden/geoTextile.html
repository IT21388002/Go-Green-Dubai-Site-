GEO TEXTILE

Go Green Geo Textiles are made from 100% pure coir, offering a fully natural and biodegradable solution for soil erosion control and vegetation growth on slopes and banks. These coir-based geo textiles are versatile and can be produced using either automated or hand-operated machines, depending on project requirements. Available in various weights, the most common types are 400g/sqm and 700g/sqm, but Go Green can supply geo textiles with weights ranging from 300g/sqm to 1000g/sqm by adjusting the type and number of twines used.

Advantages of Using Go Green Geo Textiles:

1. Erosion Control: Geo textiles serve as a temporary cover for soil, protecting it from wind and water erosion until re-vegetation is established.

2. Seed and Soil Protection: They act as a barrier to hold compost material, seeds, and seed links in place, enhancing plant establishment and soil stability.

3. Soil Reinforcement:By reinforcing the soil structure, geo textiles help to prevent soil movement, especially on slopes and banks, reducing the risk of landslides and erosion.

4. Water Drainage:Coir geo textiles promote efficient water drainage, preventing waterlogging and encouraging healthy root growth and soil aeration.

5. High Tensile Strength:These textiles provide a robust structure capable of withstanding significant pressure, making them suitable for use on steep slopes and high-gradient areas.

6. Environmental Benefits: Geo textiles are fully biodegradable and improve soil quality as they decompose over time. They are made from renewable resources and contribute to sustainable land management practices.

7. Customizable Aperture Size:The aperture size (the number of yarns per given length in both directions) can be customized to suit specific applications, ensuring the textile meets project-specific needs.

8. Available in Various Sizes:Go Green Geo Textiles are available in variable sizes, depending on the project requirements. They can be supplied in roll or bale form, and customization is available based on customer preferences.

Applications of Go Green Geo Textiles:

◼ Soil Erosion Control: Effective for preventing soil erosion on riverbanks, slopes, and high-gradient areas, especially where re-vegetation is needed.

◼ Soil Reinforcement and Stability: Used to reinforce and stabilize soil in agricultural fields, road embankments, and construction sites, ensuring long-term soil health and erosion resistance.

◼ Water Drainage Projects:Ideal for projects that require efficient water drainage, such as landscaping, agricultural drainage, and slope stabilization.

Product Customization:
Go Green Geo Textiles can be supplied in a variety of sizes and weights to suit specific project needs. The textiles are available in roll or bale form, depending on customer preferences. Each product can be customized to adjust the yarn aperture size and weight per square meter, providing flexibility for different soil types and erosion control applications. For further inquiries and guidance on selecting the right geo textile for your project, our team is available to provide expert advice and recommendations.
