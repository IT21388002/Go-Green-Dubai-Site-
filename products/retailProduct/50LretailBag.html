50L RETAIL BAG

The GO GREEN 50L Bag is a premium coco peat-based growing medium, designed for modern cultivation techniques. Buffered using calcium nitrate, it offers low potassium content and an exceptionally low electrical conductivity (EC) level, making it ideal for various growing conditions. This 100% natural product provides excellent water absorption while maintaining the crucial air-to-water ratio needed for strong root growth. Its treated composition eliminates concerns about harmful fungi and insects, ensuring a hazard-free growing environment.

Advantages of GO GREEN 50L Bag:

1. Optimal Air-Water Ratio: The balanced composition ensures perfect aeration and porosity, supporting robust root development and healthy plant growth.

2. Enhanced Water Retention and Nutrient Release: With its high water retention capacity and slow nutrient release, this medium ensures continuous plant growth and strong root formation.

3. Improved Disease and Pest Resistance:The treated material prevents the development of harmful fungi and insects, reducing the need for chemical interventions.

4. Ideal pH Stability: The product offers a stable pH level, avoiding issues related to acidity, making it safe for a wide range of crops.

5. Low Electrical Conductivity (EC): The low EC level makes the GO GREEN 50L Bag an excellent choice for seed germination and root development, particularly in hydroponic systems and seed planting.

6. Eco-Friendly and Sustainable: This biodegradable medium contains low levels of potassium, sodium, chloride, and tannins, contributing to a more sustainable growing environment.

Recommended Applications for GO GREEN 50L Bag:

◼ Vegetable Cultivation: Ideal for growing vegetables in both soil-based and soilless systems.

◼ Fruit Cultivation: Supports strong root development, promoting healthier fruit production.

◼ Hydroponics:Its low EC level and high porosity make it an excellent choice for hydroponic setups.

◼ Potting Mix: Suitable for use as a standalone potting mix or as part of a customized blend.

◼ Cannabis Cultivation: Provides optimal growing conditions, supporting rapid growth and strong root systems.

◼ Seed Planting:Its low EC and stable pH make it perfect for seed starting and early-stage root development.

The GO GREEN 50L Bag is designed to enhance plant growth across various conditions while maintaining eco-friendliness, making it suitable for both commercial and home gardening applications.
