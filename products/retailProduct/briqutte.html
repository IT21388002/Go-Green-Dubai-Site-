BRIQUTTE

GO GREEN Coco Husk Chips Briquettes are made from 100% coco coarse material, compressed into hard 500g bricks. These briquettes are an ideal medium for cultivating flowering plants such as orchids and anthuriums. Upon hydration and expansion, each briquette yields up to 7 liters of growing medium, providing an organic, biodegradable substrate that offers excellent aeration and long-lasting water retention.

Advantages of Husk Chips Briquettes:

1. Optimal Air-Water Balance: The coarse structure of the coco husk chips ensures a perfect balance between aeration and moisture retention, promoting healthier root development and robust plant growth.

2. Long-Lasting Moisture Retention: With its high water retention capacity, the briquette reduces the risk of unwanted fungal growth, which can harm flowering plants. This creates a healthier environment for steady, uninterrupted plant growth.

3. Strong Rooting Support:The coarse particles provide an excellent grip for plant roots, leading to stronger root systems and enhancing overall plant stability.

4. Organic and Biodegradable: The briquettes are 100% organic and biodegradable, making them an environmentally friendly choice for sustainable agriculture.

5. pH Stability: With a stable pH level of 5.5–6.5, the briquettes are suitable for a wide range of plants, including both vegetables and flowers. This makes them versatile for various cultivation needs.

Recommended Applications for GO GREEN Coco Husk Chips Briquettes:

◼ Flower Cultivation: Particularly suited for growing orchids and anthuriums, ensuring a healthy root structure and optimal growth conditions.

◼ Vegetable Cultivation: Can be used for a variety of vegetable plants due to its stable pH and high water retention properties.

◼ Greenhouses and Nurseries:Ideal for both commercial and home use, providing a reliable and eco-friendly growing medium.

◼ Customized Packaging: The product can be packed according to your specific needs and even with private labeling options, making it adaptable for different market requirements.

GO GREEN Coco Husk Chips Briquettes provide a sustainable, high-quality solution for flower and vegetable growers, offering long-term moisture retention, strong root support, and environmental benefits.
