BRUSH/ MATS/ BROOM

Coir Brushes, Mats, and Brooms are high-quality products crafted from tightly bound natural palm fiber or coco fiber. These items are designed for durability and environmental friendliness, making them ideal for a range of domestic and commercial cleaning and decorative purposes. Some products are also made with polypropylene (PP) fiber in various colors, adding to their versatility and appeal.

Advantages of Coir Brushes, Mats, and Brooms:

1. Durability and Strength: Coir Brushes and Door Mats are made from strong and durable natural palm or coco fibers, ensuring that these products are long-lasting and effective in their intended use. The tightly bound fibers contribute to their robust construction, making them suitable for heavy-duty cleaning tasks and regular use.

2. Environmental Sustainability: All coir products are environmentally friendly, made from natural, renewable resources. This makes them a sustainable choice for consumers looking to reduce their environmental impact without compromising on quality.

3. Versatile Range of Products: The range includes various types of brushes, mats, and brooms, each tailored to meet specific customer requirements. Coir Brushes, also known as Tawashi Brushes, are perfect for cleaning vegetables and small crevices, while Bottle Brushes and Handle Brushes are ideal for cleaning bottles, pans, and bowls. Toilet Brushes are designed for efficient toilet cleaning, helping maintain hygiene in bathrooms.

4. Customizable and Convenient: Coir products are available in a variety of sizes and designs, allowing for customization according to customer specifications. Door Mats and Coir Mats, for instance, are available in different sizes and designs, produced from coir brush rods with durable steel frames for added strength and longevity.

5. Retail and Bulk Availability: Whether for large-scale commercial use or individual retail, these products can be supplied in bulk or smaller quantities, catering to different market needs. Packaging can be customized according to the customer's requirements, ensuring convenience and suitability for various distribution channels.

Specific Applications:

1. Coir Brushes (Tawashi Brushes): Ideal for cleaning vegetables and small crevices in bowls, offering a comfortable grip and effective cleaning performance. Available in various weights and sizes (e.g., 40g, 50g, 75g, 110g; 3”, 4”, 6”).

2. Bottle and Handle Brushes: Essential for cleaning bottles, pans, and bowls, providing thorough cleaning with minimal effort.

3. Toilet Brushes: Designed to efficiently clean toilets, maintaining hygiene and cleanliness with durable construction.

4. Brooms and Banister Brushes: Effective for sweeping and cleaning, combining natural fiber strength with practical design.

5. Door Mats and Coir Mats: Available in a wide range of designs, these mats are natural, durable, and retain their visual appeal longer than synthetic alternatives, making them a preferred choice for both functional and decorative use.

Coir Brushes, Mats, and Brooms offer a natural, durable, and eco-friendly solution for various cleaning and decorative needs. With their robust construction, versatility, and sustainability, these products are a valuable addition to any home or commercial setting.
